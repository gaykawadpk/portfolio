The Science of Deduction (and Debugging)

The clues are in the stack trace, Watson,
each line a thread I mustn't loosen,
I pace the floor of Baker Street,
where logic and the errors meet.

Eliminate the impossible first—
that missing comma was the worst.
The truth, improbable but clear:
the bug was in line two, my dear.

elementary

All Too Well (in Production)

I remember it all too well—
the autumn night the servers fell,
the dashboard red, the Slack on fire,
my coffee cold, the stakes much higher.

You kept the merge request like a secret,
I kept the hotfix like a promise.
"We are never ever getting back together"—
me, to that legacy codebase. Forever.

The Hound of the Baskervilles (But It's a Bug)

a race condition I had missed.

an off-by-one. The case finds end.

A Study in Light

Watson never understood why I
would stand for hours, camera high,
and wait for light to crack the sky
like truth through an elaborate lie.

I'll show you beauty in the plain.
